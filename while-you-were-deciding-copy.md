# While You Were Deciding — Full Copy & Engagement Strategy

**App concept:** User inputs a big life decision + date + why it felt enormous.
The app reveals world events at that moment and generates a shareable "Mission Log" card.
Closing line: "The world didn't hold its breath. It never does. You decided anyway."

---

## 1. LANDING PAGE COPY — 3 Hero Section Variants

---

### VARIANT A — Existential / Philosophical
**Tone:** Quiet, grounding, slightly unsettling in the best way.
**Target emotion:** Awe + perspective

**Headline:**
> Your biggest decision happened on a perfectly ordinary day.

**Subheadline:**
> Somewhere else, a 5.2 earthquake was rattling dishes off shelves. The ISS was passing over the Sahara at 17,500 mph. A near-Earth asteroid came and went without ceremony. You had no idea. Neither did anyone else. And you decided anyway.

**CTA Button:**
> Enter Your Decision

**Below-fold supporting line (optional):**
> Every private turning point exists inside a universe in full motion. This is what yours looked like.

---

### VARIANT B — Playful / Curious
**Tone:** Light, inviting, wonder-driven — feels like a game, not a therapy session.
**Target emotion:** Curiosity + delight

**Headline:**
> What was the universe doing the day you changed your life?

**Subheadline:**
> Spoiler: it was a lot. Floods, asteroids, news cycles, orbital mechanics — the whole show. While you were sweating your decision, the cosmos had a full schedule. Let's review the tape.

**CTA Button:**
> Find Out What Happened

**Below-fold supporting line (optional):**
> It takes about 60 seconds. You've already spent longer on lesser things today.

---

### VARIANT C — Dramatic / Cinematic
**Tone:** Mission-briefing, documentary voiceover energy. Feels like a trailer.
**Target emotion:** Drama + personal significance

**Headline:**
> On the day you made your move, the world kept moving too.

**Subheadline:**
> You didn't know what was happening at the edges of the earth when you made that call. This is the complete record. The news. The tremors. The satellites overhead. Your decision, placed inside the moment it was actually made.

**CTA Button:**
> Open the Mission Log

**Below-fold supporting line (optional):**
> Your decision deserves context. Here is the universe's side of the story.

## 6. EASTER EGGS + DELIGHT MOMENTS

### Edge Case: User enters today's date

**Message:**
> You're deciding right now?
>
> The record is being written as we speak. Come back when it's done. The universe will still have been moving.

---

### Edge Case: User enters their birthday

**Detection note:** If date matches a birthday field (if you collect one), or if user types "my birthday" in the decision field.

**Message:**
> That's the day you arrived. Everything in the field conditions section was happening the moment you entered the world. Think of it less as a decision and more as a debut.

---

### Edge Case: User enters a future date

**Message:**
> That hasn't happened yet. The record is blank.
>
> Come back after. We'll be here.

---

### Edge Case: Empty or near-empty input fields

**If "The Decision" field is submitted blank:**
> Even indecision is a decision. But we need a little more to work with.

---

### Edge Case: A historically significant date entered (e.g., 9/11, COVID lockdown start)

**Message (displayed above the card):**
> You know this day. A lot of people do. Your decision lived inside it too.

---

### 404 Page

**Headline:**
> This page doesn't exist.

**Body:**
> Which is fine. Most of the universe is also empty space, and it's doing great.

**CTA:**
> Go Back to the Record

## 7. EMAIL / NEWSLETTER STRATEGY

### Feature Concept: "Decision of the Week"

**Format:** Weekly email featuring one anonymized, user-submitted Mission Log.
Sent to subscribers who opt in at the end of their own Mission Log experience.

**Opt-in prompt (end of Mission Log card view):**
> Want to read someone else's?
>
> Every week we publish one anonymized Mission Log — someone else's decision, their date, the world that was happening around them. No names. Just the moment.
>
> [Subscribe — it's one email a week, nothing else]

---

### Newsletter Name Options

1. The Mission Log
2. Field Conditions
3. While You Were Deciding (same as app, as a newsletter)
4. One Decision, One Week
5. The Record

**Recommended:** "Field Conditions" — it's distinctive, on-brand, and doesn't sound like a newsletter.
